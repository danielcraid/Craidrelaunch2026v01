### IMPORTANT DESIGN NOTES FOR THE BUILDER:

1. The overall positioning is TRANSFORMATION CONSULTANCY, not AI agency. CRAiD helps organisations transform – they don't build agents for clients.
2. The AI agents in the team section should feel like a natural part of how CRAiD works (behind-the-scenes), NOT like a product being sold.
3. The Doro section should feel like a proof point of CRAiD's way of working, not like a product demo.
4. Services should clearly communicate outcomes and business value, not just activities.
5. The Delivery Factory (Service 04) should feel distinct – more bold, more production-oriented. Consider a slightly different visual treatment to make it stand out.
6. Value drivers in Service 02 (Growth · Churn · Service · Efficiency) should be visually prominent – these are the reason clients hire CRAiD.
7. Keep the dark, premium aesthetic. Purple/magenta accent colors. Clean typography.
